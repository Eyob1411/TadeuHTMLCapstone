Pata schule works in four simple steps
1-Search

It's simple. On Pata shule homepage, enter the name of a school you're looking for aand click on search. Alternatively click on the search link on the top menu to view the list of all schools.

2-Locate

Get to the see where the school is located by clicking on the school profile. It's even more exciting. You can get the direction from anywhere in the country!

3-Apply

Apply for your child's interview to the school of your choice. Click on the APPLY button to get the application form. We also follow up on the application to make sure you get a reply on time.

4-Relax

Once your child gets admitted. You can now relax. You can check out our blog to see what we recomend when school hunting
